**1\. General Conduct & Ethics (For Everyone)**

1\. Respectful Behaviour:

Maintain respect toward all students, staff, guests, and crew.

No bullying, harassment, mocking, or abusive language.

2\. Professionalism:

Be punctual for all events.

Follow event heads' instructions without argument.

3\. Cleanliness:

Maintain cleanliness at all venues. Use dustbins.

Damaging property will lead to penalties.

4\. ID Requirement:

Event volunteers must wear ID badges visibly.

Event participants must wear ID badges visibly.

5\. No Alcohol / Drugs:

Strictly prohibited inside premises. Immediate removal if found.

6\. No Weapon / Dangerous Objects:

Any harmful item will result in disciplinary action. Immediate removal if found.

**2\. Opposite Gender Interaction & Premise Rules**

These rules maintain safety, comfort, and discipline:

1\. Restricted Areas:

No student is allowed in hostel areas of the opposite gender, backstage dressing rooms, or any private prep rooms unless officially assigned.

Entry is only allowed with permission from event heads.

2\. Physical Conduct:

Maintain appropriate physical boundaries.

No intimate behaviour or inappropriate touching.

**3\. Recording Consent:**

Taking photos/videos of anyone without consent is forbidden.

Especially in backstage, makeup, green room, or preparation areas.

4\. Zero Tolerance Harassment Policy:

Any harassment complaint (verbal, visual, physical, digital) leads to immediate removal from the event and disciplinary actions

3\. Management / Organising Team Rules

1\. Authority & Responsibility:

Team heads' decisions are final.

All volunteers must follow duty schedules strictly.

2\. Communication Etiquette:

Use respectful language with participants.

Never shout or insult any participant publicly.

3\. Neutrality:

Volunteers must remain fair - no favoritism.

4\. Safety Priority:

Report any misbehavior, injuries, or suspicious activity instantly to the core team.

5\. Confidential Spaces:

Opposite genders are not allowed in restricted green rooms or changing areas.

Only authorized persons are allowed backstage.

**4\. Event-Specific Rules**

A. Painting

1\. Your artwork must reflect the theme clearly.

2\. Cartridge drawing sheet will be provided by the organizers.

3\. Participants must bring their own colors, brushes, palette, etc.

4\. All painting mediums are allowed. Participants may use any colors or materials of their choice.

5\. The time limit must be respected.

6\. No plagiarism; must be original artwork.

B. Sculpturing

1\. Base and clay will be provided by the organizers .

2\. Tools to be carried by the participants .

3\. Use safe tools only; sharp/hazardous items restricted.

4\. Maintain workspace cleanliness.

5.. Your artwork must reflect the theme clearly.

6\. The time limit must be respected.

C. Rangoli

1\. Rangoli and colours will be provided by the organizers.

2\. Pre-made stencils are not allowed unless event permits.

3\. Work only within the marked area.

4\. No external help during competition.

5\. Your artwork must reflect the theme clearly.

6\. The time limit must be respected.

D. Group Dance

1\. Open to registered teams only.

2\. Team size: 6-12 members

3\. Every participant must belong to the youth festival's age criteria.

4\. The performance must be a Folk, Classical, or Semi-Classical dance showcasing Indian culture and tradition.

5\. No vulgar or inappropriate movements; maintain cultural respect.

6\. Performance duration: 5-8 minutes including entry and exit.

( other details will be emailed or conveyed over a call to the dance coordinator of the team. )

D. Hackathon

1\. Participants must follow problem statement guidelines.

2\. No internet access unless allowed.

3\. The project must be original.

4\. The group must stay within the designated working area.

5\. Plagiarism or use of pre-built code leads to disqualification.

E. Talk Show / Podcast

1\. Content must be clean and respectful.

2\. No abusive language or controversial/politically sensitive topics.

3\. Follow time limits strictly.

F. Reel Making

1\. Reel must follow the given theme.

2\. No inappropriate content, vulgarity, or unsafe acts.

3\. Copyright-free music recommended.

4\. Must be shot within campus unless rules specify otherwise.

G. Game Zone

1\. Follow game-specific instructions.

2\. No arguing with coordinators.

3\. No cheating or misusing equipment.

4\. Damaged items must be compensated.

H. Food Corners

1\. Maintain hygiene.

2\. No food waste near activity areas.

3\. Avoid crowding; follow the queue system.

**5\. Safety & Security Rules**

1\. Emergency exits must be kept clear.

2\. In case of fire/alarm, follow volunteer instructions.

3\. Any medical emergency must be reported instantly.

6\. Disqualification Rules

A participant/team may be disqualified for:

● Breaking event rules

● Misbehaving with opposite gender

● Cheating, plagiarism, or fraud

● Interfering with management work

● Disruptive or abusive behavior

**7\. Consent & Liability**

1\. Participants agree that the event may record photos/videos publicly.

2\. The organizing team is not responsible for lost personal belongings.

3\. Participants must sign the rule acknowledgment form at registration.

**Note : Organizers reserve all rights to make changes. Decisions by organizers are final.**
