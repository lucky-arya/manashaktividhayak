General Guidelines

General Conduct & Ethics

  • Respectful Behaviour: Maintain respect toward all students, staff, guests, and crew. No bullying, harassment, mocking, or abusive language.
  • Professionalism: Be punctual for all events. Follow event heads' instructions without argument.
  • Cleanliness: Maintain cleanliness at all venues. Use dustbins. Damaging property will lead to penalties.
  • ID Requirement: Event volunteers and participants must wear ID badges visibly.
  • No Alcohol/Drugs: Strictly prohibited inside premises. Immediate removal if found.
  • No Weapons/Dangerous Objects: Any harmful item will result in disciplinary action and immediate removal.

Opposite Gender Interaction & Premise Rules

  • Restricted Areas: No student is allowed in hostel areas of the opposite gender, backstage dressing rooms, or any private prep rooms unless officially assigned. Entry is only allowed with permission from event heads.
  • Physical Conduct: Maintain appropriate physical boundaries. No intimate behaviour or inappropriate touching.
  • Recording Consent: Taking photos/videos of anyone without consent is forbidden, especially in backstage, makeup, green room, or preparation areas.
  • Zero Tolerance Harassment Policy: Any harassment complaint (verbal, visual, physical, digital) leads to immediate removal from the event and disciplinary actions.

Safety & Security

  • Emergency exits must be kept clear.
  • In case of fire/alarm, follow volunteer instructions.
  • Any medical emergency must be reported instantly.

Disqualification Rules

  • Breaking event rules
  • Misbehaving with opposite gender
  • Cheating, plagiarism, or fraud
  • Interfering with management work
  • Disruptive or abusive behavior

Consent & Liability

  • Participants agree that the event may record photos/videos publicly.
  • The organizing team is not responsible for lost personal belongings.
  • Participants must sign the rule acknowledgment form at registration.

Note: Organizers reserve all rights to make changes. Decisions by organizers are final.
